Week 2: thought question demo

Instructions

This week's thought questions involves taking part in a quick experiment! You're going to pretend to be a monkey for a moment and take part in a classic motion detection experiment. You heard about this experiment in the work of Bill Newsome presented in lecture.

You will see a patch of dots moving randomly. Press the up key to start a trial: after a brief delay (200 ms) a small percentage of the dots will begin moving right or left. As soon as you detect which direction the dots are going, press the corresponding key, left or right. Before starting the thought question you should do a practice run. After 20 trials you will be shown your reaction time and percent correct. Refresh the page between each run (to reset the data collection). Press the mode button to switch between high coherence and low coherence.

Mode: High coherence
